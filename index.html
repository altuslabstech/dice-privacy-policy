Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dice, developed by Altus Labs, handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

Dice does not require account registration or login, and we do not directly collect personal information from users through the app.

2. Advertising

Dice may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device identifiers, usage data, and advertising-related information to serve and measure ads. You can learn more about how Google handles data here: https://policies.google.com/privacy .

3. Permissions

Dice may request access to vibration features solely to provide haptic feedback when rolling dice. No other device permissions are used for core functionality.

4. Data Storage

App settings such as sound, vibration, theme, and display preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this information to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Dice uses Google AdMob for advertising. Third-party services may process data according to their own privacy policies. Please review Google’s Privacy Policy for more details: https://policies.google.com/privacy .

6. Children’s Privacy

Dice is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
